Grant Proposal for a Korean Heritage Program for Adult Adoptees (PDF)
This 24-page grant proposal was a final project for a Professional Writing and Technical Communication course. It highlights the need for a Korean heritage program for adult adoptees and offers a structured program with events, locations, and pricing.

Microsoft Word for Dietitians (PDF)
I created this user manual for a final deliverable in a Professional Writing and Technical Communication course. It is catered towards dietitians and other healthcare professionals and explains step-by-step multiple Microsoft Word (on PC) functions.

The Economic Effects of Climate Change on New Zealand’s Pastoral Agriculture (PDF)
Climate change is one of the most concerning environmental, social, and political issues of our world, and its various and abundant negative effects are becoming more prevalent in our lives. This concise 9-page research paper discusses the economic effects climate change has on New Zealand’s pastoral agriculture, which is one of the country’s leading industries that contributes to GDP and HDI.

Climate Change Mitigation Presentation (PDF)
A classmate and I created this presentation for a course on environmental and resource economics. There is a shorter version but I have linked the long version. In this presentation, we discuss climate change mitigation policy and investment in clean energy.
